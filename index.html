Welcome to the showcase website!

Hello there! My name is Nikolay Favorsky and this is my showcase website. Its purpose is to introduce myself to you in the most memorable way. Please feel free to read, use or even distribute any information you can find here).

There are multiple reasons why I chose to study HBO-ICT in HZ University of Applied Sciences:

  • I assume that is unknowable now, but for whatever reason in this spring my registration for the only exam in Russia for higher education was canceled. When I realized that, it was too late to appeal. That means I was forced to search options for abroad study. Otherwise I would had to serve in Russian Army for a year or so... Due to the fact, that I did not plan to study abroad, there were only a few programs to choose from.

  • It was the only program that is able to forge a software engineer. But why software engineering in the first place? I have a dream to become a video game developer at some point. From my point of view, only a proficient software engineer can bind all the required pieces of art into a single one in order to acomplish an ambitious project like one I dream about.

  • All of that may sound like I am a bit disgusted by the fact, that I did not have a choice. But to be honest, I am glad my fate ended up bringing me here. Only for the last 2 weeks I've been working on a project with more enthusiasm than I have ever experienced.

I have no doubt that this program suits me perfectly. The only thing left is to prove that to you).

Which might take a while..
