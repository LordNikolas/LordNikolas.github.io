Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

---> There is at least one printer on every floor and each can be accessed with a student pass.

2. How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

---> Same device and same requirements.

3. What do you need to do when you are sick / show symptoms of coronavirus?

---> Isolate yourself from others, notify the Student Office and follow the classes remotely via BigBlueButton and Notion.

4. How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

---> A student can book a room on the HZ Selfservice Portal.

5. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

---> Free parking is available at the car park of PZEM at Poelendaelesingel 10 in Middelburg.
